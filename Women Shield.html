Women Shield

Your Safety, Our Priority. Dedicated features and tips for women travelers.

The "Women Shield" is our dedicated initiative to enhance road safety and security for women. This includes special in-app features and essential knowledge to help you travel with confidence.

Core Safety Features

Share My Ride

Easily share your live trip status, vehicle details, and ETA with family or trusted contacts in a single tap.

Emergency SOS

An accessible SOS button to discreetly alert our 24/7 response team and local authorities if you feel unsafe.

Vetted Drivers

Ride with confidence. Our "Women Shield" program ensures drivers pass enhanced background checks and training.

Trusted Contacts

Set up your emergency contacts in the app. They will be automatically notified when you trigger an SOS alert.

Proactive Safety Tips

Awareness in Car Parks

Always park in well-lit areas, preferably close to entrances or security cameras. Before getting out or returning to your car, be aware of your surroundings. Have your keys in hand, and check the backseat before entering your vehicle. Avoid being distracted by your phone.

Vehicle Maintenance

A breakdown in an unsafe or remote area can be a significant risk. Regularly check your vehicle's fuel, tire pressure, and oil. Ensure your phone is fully charged before any long trip, and consider keeping a portable power bank in your car.

Share Your Plans

Whether you're driving or taking a ride-share, let a trusted person know your route, destination, and expected arrival time (ETA). Use the "Share My Ride" feature whenever possible, especially when traveling at night or in unfamiliar areas.

Trust Your Instincts

If a person or situation feels unsafe, it probably is. If you feel you are being followed, drive to a public, well-lit place like a police station or a 24-hour gas station. Do not drive straight home. If you're in a ride-share and feel uncomfortable, ask the driver to stop in a safe, public area and end the ride.
