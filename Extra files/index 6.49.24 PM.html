Essential Road Education

What is Defensive Driving?
Using safe driving strategies to anticipate hazards, look beyond the vehicle in front, and avoid crashes by constantly scanning the environment.
Recognising Road Hazards
Be vigilant for poor visibility (fog, rain), unexpected pedestrians, aggressive or distracted drivers, and road surface changes (potholes, ice).

Quick Safety Tips

Always wear your seatbelt
The single most effective safety feature. It reduces serious injury and death in accidents by over 50%.
Don’t text and drive
Distracted driving is a major cause of crashes. If you must use your phone, pull over safely or use a hands-free system.
